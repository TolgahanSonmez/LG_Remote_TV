Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2025

Introduction

LG TV Remote ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The App operates entirely on your local network and does not communicate with any external servers. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers
  • No data is sent to third parties
  • No account creation required

Local Network Access

The App requires access to your local Wi-Fi network solely to discover and communicate with smart TVs on the same network. This communication happens directly between your device and your TV — no data passes through our servers or any third-party service.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your TV's IP address for auto-reconnection
  • App preferences (such as dark mode setting)

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
